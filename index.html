About GuardPilot

GuardPilot is a smart autonomous drone security system equipped with AI modules, real-time patrol monitoring, and mobile app control. Simple integration and instant alerts for your safety.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Launch drone patrol via mobile app.
  • Step 2: Real-time monitoring and AI-based threat detection.
  • Step 3: Instant alerts and optional manual intervention.
  • Step 4: Automatic reporting and patrol logs.

Mobile App

Control GuardPilot directly from your smartphone: live video, real-time notifications, patrol scheduling, and manual override controls all in one easy-to-use interface.
